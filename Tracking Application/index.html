The App


With a simple interface its easy to keep customer information, orders, and inventory together. This helps keep track of product flow and avoiding product expiration. Adding items to customer orders and inventory using our systems is simple, intuitive and available anywhere you can connect to the internet!




The Technology


Using Firebase and Android, we have built an app that is as secure as it is easy to use. The information entered into the app is sent through a secure connection and is encrypted and saved in the Firebase servers, meaning that the only people that can access your data are the people that you let in. Since both Firebase and Android are developed by Google, they are able to work together better than most other platforms allowing a faster and more responsive application.
